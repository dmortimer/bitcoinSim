Bitcoin

Bitcoin is a cryptocurrency and a digital payment system released as open-source software in 2009. Bitcoin is a peer-to-peer system where transactions take place between users directly, without an intermediary. Since the system works without a central repository or single administrator, bitcoin is called the first decentralized digital currency.
